How the Process Works

Clear steps reduce uncertainty, especially when buying property in Kigali, selling property in Rwanda, or managing a transaction from abroad.

  1. Contact MENU

    Tell us whether you want to buy, sell, rent, lease, or understand your options.

  2. Property consultation

    We clarify budget, location, property type, timeline, and the decision-makers involved.

  3. Search or valuation

    Buyers receive suitable options; sellers receive pricing guidance and preparation advice.

  4. Site visits

    We coordinate viewings, document questions, and help compare strengths and risks.

  5. Negotiation

    We support offer strategy, payment expectations, timelines, and practical deal terms.

  6. Verification

    We help coordinate document review, ownership checks, and legal due diligence support.

  7. Transfer or lease

    We guide the transaction toward signing, payment milestones, handover, and records.

  8. After-sale support

    We remain available for next steps such as management, valuation, improvements, or resale planning.
